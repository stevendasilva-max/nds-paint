Meet Niro DaSilva

Niro DaSilva is the founder and heart of NDS Fine Residential Painting. Born in Brazil, Niro brought his passion for craftsmanship to the United States over 25 years ago — and has been transforming homes ever since. A former pastor in both Brazil and the United States, Niro leads his business with the same integrity, care, and dedication he brought to the pulpit.

As a husband, father, and home owner, he understands what a home truly means to a family — and every project reflects that understanding. NDS began in the greater Boston, Massachusetts area, where Niro built a reputation for meticulous work and lasting relationships; in 2020 the business relocated to Oklahoma, and today NDS proudly serves Edmond and the greater Oklahoma City area.

Edmond is home to a vibrant community; learn more about the city from the official City of Edmond website.
